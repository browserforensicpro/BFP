FAQ
Why READ-ONLY mode? ▼
BFP never writes to browser data, ensuring forensic integrity. The only exception is "Clear Cache" which requires explicit confirmation.
Why can't I recover incognito history? ▼
Chrome and Edge permanently delete incognito history on window close. Use Incognito Detect for artifact-based indicators instead.
How do I change the timestamp timezone? ▼
Go to Settings → Time & Format → Timezone. Select your preferred timezone (IST, UTC, PST, etc.) and click Save Settings. All timestamps update immediately — no reload needed.
App hangs after extended use — what to do? ▼
A 30-second timeout is built in. If the spinner never stops, click the Refresh button at the top-right. This reloads the current profile without restarting the app.
How is feedback delivered? ▼
When you click Submit Feedback, Gmail opens in your browser with your message pre-filled and addressed to the developer. Just click Send in Gmail. A backup copy is also saved locally in the app temp folder.
